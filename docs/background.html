Playing with Linear Gradients

This website is meant to act as a showcase of my projects, and this is one of them! I created this website mostly in a day, with some intial help from the odd LLM. However, when I was deciding what to do as a background, I started messing around with Linear Gradients, and at around 2am, I stumbled upon the idea of layering these partially transparent gradients together at different rotations, leading to the thorny looking background you see here, which is just a tiling of 4 tiny gradients that form little triangles, alonside some smart colour choices and positioning. I actually spent a few hours trying out different patterns, and in the end I decided that I would add a toy on the website just to appreciate some of the cool patterns you can make, and let you mess around with all the numbers! Enjoy!
