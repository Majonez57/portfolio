...who?

I'm Adrian, a PhD student with the CHART research group at the University of Nottingham.

I'm interested in all things robotic, specifically anything that brings robots closer to people! This includes robotic embodiment design, control algorithms, and especially the applications of Machine Learning to improve Human-Robot Collaboration. Right now I'm looking into multimodal applications of Learning from Demonstration (LfD), using methods to allow robots to learn from us more effectively, making working alongside robots more intuitive and easy for people. I'm really curious to investigate how these methods can allow human feedback to direcly affect the autonomous actions of a robot.
